Curriculum

AI Learning Modules

What secondary school students will learn about Artificial Intelligence — from everyday tools to the ethics of AI.

Lesson 1

AI Tools

Discover the AI-powered tools students use every day — from search engines and voice assistants to writing helpers and study apps. Learn how to choose and use them wisely.

Lesson 2

Intro to Machine Learning

Understand how machines learn from data. Explore patterns, training, and prediction — the building blocks behind recommendation systems, spam filters, and more.

Lesson 3

Computer Vision

How do computers "see"? Learn about image recognition, face detection, and object identification — the technology behind self-driving cars, medical imaging, and photo filters.

Lesson 4

Chatbots & Large Language Models

Explore how AI understands and generates text. From customer service bots to ChatGPT — learn how large language models work and how to use them responsibly.

Lesson 5

Generative Images

Create images with AI. Understand how tools like DALL·E and Midjourney turn text into pictures, and learn about creativity, copyright, and deepfakes.

Lesson 6

Algorithmic Bias

Why can AI be unfair? Learn how bias enters algorithms, its real-world impact, and how we can build and use AI more ethically and inclusively.
